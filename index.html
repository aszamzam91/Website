Bio

I'm a PhD Candidate at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Minnesota. Also, I am affiliated with Signal and Tensor Analytics Research (STAR) group under the supervision of Professor Nikos D. Sidiropoulos. Previously, I earned my BSc from the Department of Electronics and Electrical Communications Engineering at ﻿Cairo University﻿ in 2013. In 2015, I received my MSc from Nile University in Wireless Communications. From 2013 to 2015, I was working as a research assistant at Wireless Intelligent Networks Center at Nile University.

I received the Louis John Schnell Fellowship (2015), the Doctoral Dissertation Fellowship (2018) from the University of Minnesota. I also received the Student Travel Awards from IEEE Signal Processing Society (2017), IEEE Power and Energy Society (2018), and Council of Graduate Students at the University of Minnesota (2016, 2018).

Research Interests

  • Large-Scale Complex Energy Systems
